Personas

Mariah Bells

  • Job Title: Stay at home mom
  • Demographics: She's 30 years old and married with one five year old boy. She has two dogs, a four year old golden retriever and a two year old husky. She lives in a small town house with a fenced in backyard. She has a degree in accounting, but currently is not employed.
  • Goals and tasks: She is trying to figure out if the weather will be nice enough to leave her dogs out back for the day. She is also wondering if the weather will be nice enough to take her dogs for a walk.
  • Environment: She meets up with other moms for playdates with kids and dogs. She has a computer at home, good wifi, and a new apple phone. She is reasonably comfortable using the Internet, but she does prefer reading physical books and using paper copies.
  • Quote: “Are we gonna play outside or inside today Bella?”

Julie Morris

  • Job Title: Full-time college student
  • Demographics: Julie is 20 years old and has been dating her boyfriend for two years now. They recently got a puppy together, Bernie, who is a 10-month-old bernedoodle. They live in an apartment together. She is working on getting a degree in business management.
  • Goals and tasks: She’s new to owning a dog and wants to know if it will be too cold or hot for her pup. She also lives in an apartment so she wants to watch for rain and snow that might make her pup dirty and could dirty up her apartment.
  • Environment: She spends a lot of time with her boyfriend and her puppy. She has a laptop and smart phone and good wifi for schooling. She is very comfortable with technology and can navigate a website well.
  • Quote: “Is today a good day to take Bernie for a walk?”
