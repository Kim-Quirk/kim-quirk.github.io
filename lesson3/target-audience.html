Target Audience Description

This website is built for dog owners. People who pamper their pooches and want to know the weather for their dog’s needs. It’s for people who want to avoid taking their dogs to muddy areas after a rainstorm and for people who want to know if it’s getting warmer and if their pooch needs a summer haircut. The target audience is dog lovers who want to do everything they can for their dogs.

Personas

Mariah Bells

  • Job Title: Stay at home mom
  • Demographics: She's 30 years old and married with one five year old boy. She has two dogs, a four year old golden retriever and a two year old husky. She lives in a small town house with a fenced in backyard. She has a degree in accounting, but currently is not employed.
  • Goals and tasks: She is trying to figure out if the weather will be nice enough to leave her dogs out back for the day. She is also wondering if the weather will be nice enough to take her dogs for a walk.
  • Environment: She meets up with other moms for playdates with kids and dogs. She has a computer at home, good wifi, and a new apple phone. She is reasonably comfortable using the Internet, but she does prefer reading physical books and using paper copies.
  • Quote: “Are we gonna play outside or inside today Bella?”

Julie Morris

  • Job Title: Full-time college student
  • Demographics: Julie is 20 years old and has been dating her boyfriend for two years now. They recently got a puppy together, Bernie, who is a 10-month-old bernedoodle. They live in an apartment together. She is working on getting a degree in business management.
  • Goals and tasks: She’s new to owning a dog and wants to know if it will be too cold or hot for her pup. She also lives in an apartment so she wants to watch for rain and snow that might make her pup dirty and could dirty up her apartment.
  • Environment: She spends a lot of time with her boyfriend and her puppy. She has a laptop and smart phone and good wifi for schooling. She is very comfortable with technology and can navigate a website well.
  • Quote: “Is today a good day to take Bernie for a walk?”

Scenarios

  1. Hector is worried about leaving his chihuahua in the car when he goes to the grocery store. He looks at Dog Days Weather to see if it will be too hot to leave his pup in the car.
  2. Amy has been putting off grooming her cockapoo over the winter months. She wanter her pup to keep warm in the cold. Now that it’s springtime, the weather keeps shifting between cold and warm. She’s not sure if it will be warm enough to give her pup a summer cut or not, so she goes to check Dog Days Weather.
  3. Jack wants to take his greyhound for a run today. However, last time it rained on them when they went running and his dog got all muddy. He doesn’t want to deal with that again. He checks Dog Days Weather to see if it will rain enough to make the ground and his pooch muddy.
  4. Sophie wants to go for a leisurely stroll with her golden retriever. She wants to know if it will be a nice day for a walk. She checks Dogs Days Weather to see what it says about dog walking.
  5. When it’s nice out at night, Hamlet likes to leave his husky out at night. The husky loves to run and sleep out under the stars. However, it’s getting colder and he wants to make sure it won’t be too cold for his pooch outside. He checks Dog Days Weather to see if it will be too cold to keep his husky outside overnight.
