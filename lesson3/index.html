Dog Days Weather

Domain Name

dogdaysweather.com

Site Purpose

A weather site built for dogs and dog owners. It will provide weather reports tailored to the needs of dog owners. For example, it will warn of hot days and freezing nights that would be dangerous to leave dogs outside it. It will also warn of rain and snow that might lead to muddy, messy pups. It would also forecast ahead to try and help dog parents predict grooming needs. For example, if it’s getting warmer consistently, the site will say it’s time to get your pooch a summer haircut. The site will also point out perfect weather for going on a walk with your pooch.
