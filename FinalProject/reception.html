Wedding Receptions

Each hotel is equipped with a large ballroom that can be reserved for a wedding reception. The room is large and spacious. It’s the perfect place for any wedding. With each hotel located only a few blocks from a Latter-day Saints Temple, it’s a short walk between the temple and the reception. Have your temple wedding and reception located close together!

The room can hold a maximum of 200 occupants. The room is large and spacious. Tables, chairs, and table cloths will be provided. Decorating will be left to the wedding party. The room is also equipped with surround sound. Once the room is secured, we’ll give you a tour of how to connect to the surround sound via Bluetooth.



Food Accommodations

While you are always welcome to bring your own food, Temple Inn & Suites can also cater for your wedding. Look over the following menu for food catering options.

There are two options. You can select one item per category (i.e., breadsticks, chicken alfredo, and brownies). These items will then be presented in a buffet-type style according to the number of guests attending the reception. The second option is you purchase a meal per guest. This will allow each guest to individually select one option from each category. With this option, we will have waiters and servers ensuring everyone receives the appropriate food. This second option is recommended for smaller receptions.
