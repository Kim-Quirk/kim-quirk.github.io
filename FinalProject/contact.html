Contact Temple Inn & Suites

Judy Karen, Temple Inn & Suites's CEO

Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.

Feel fre to contact us about any and all of your questions or concerns. If you have a reservation with a specific location, it's recommended you contact that location directly. General questions about the company would be best suited for Temple Inns & Suites main office. If you're unsure who to contact, call the main office and we can help direct you to the appropriate contact.

We'd love to hear from you!
